About Us

name is a non-profitable Organization that aims to directly benefit the underprivileged parts of our society.The proposed project strives to help those people who are in search of basic needs with the help of local community engaging in charity work. It is a setup in such a way that all contributors and beneficiaries may join up and help one another.

Generally, what we do when don't need a particular thing in our closet or in our homes? We throw it in the garbage without having a single thought.Our aim is to donate these things.We can keep track of discarded or unneeded items because we believe that what is rubbish to one person may be percious to another.We can donate these things with the help of local NGOs.For an emerging nation like INDIA,underprivileged community holds an important role in nation devlopment.Atleast they should have basic needs.We strongly beleieves that unless members of civil society are involved proactively in the process of devlopment,sustainable change will not happen.Anyone can use this website from the comfort of their home, you just need to provide some important information about yourself and list down the items you want to donate, and a local NGO of your area can pick it up according to your preffered timing and give it to someone who need that thing. It is an immense pleasure to offer someone anything that he can use . who knows, may be you will get something of equal worth in return.
